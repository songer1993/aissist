## Why Aissist?

**For Developers**: Track commits, PRs, and stand-ups automatically via GitHub integration. Generate daily reports with AI.

**For Fitness Enthusiasts**: Log workouts, track progress, and celebrate PRs (personal records, not pull requests ğŸ˜„).

**For Creators**: Manage side projects, portfolio work, and creative goals alongside your day job.

**For Everyone**: Your life is worth remembering. Aissist helps you capture, search, and reflect on what matters.

---

## Key Features Shown in This Demo:

1. **Multi-Context Management**: Separate spaces for work, fitness, personal projects - all searchable together
2. **AI-Powered Semantic Search**: Ask "what was my biggest win?" and AI finds it across all contexts
3. **Year-in-Review Reports**: Generate polished summaries for performance reviews, reflections, or social sharing
4. **Local-First Privacy**: Your data never leaves your machine
5. **Git-Friendly Storage**: All Markdown files, perfect for version control
6. **Claude AI Integration**: Uses Claude Code for semantic recall and action planning
